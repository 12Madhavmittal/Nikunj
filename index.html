Cristiano Ronaldo — Overview

Full name: Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro

Born: 5 February 1985, Funchal, Madeira, Portugal

Nicknames / Brand: CR7

Current status (as of 2025): Professional footballer, plays as forward and captains Al-Nassr (Saudi Pro League) and Portugal national football team.

Widely regarded as one of the greatest football players in history.

Early Life & Entry into Football

•Ronaldo grew up in Funchal, Madeira. His father worked as an equipment manager for a local club.

•At age 15 he was diagnosed with a heart-related condition requiring surgery — he recovered fully and returned to football.

•He started his youth career at local clubs including Sporting CP (Sporting Lisbon), making his first-team debut in 2002.

Club Career: From Sporting to Al-Nassr

Manchester United (2003–2009)

•Signed by Manchester United in 2003. At United he quickly emerged as a top young talent.

•The 2007–08 season was a breakout: he scored 42 goals across competitions, won the European Golden Shoe (as Europe’s top scorer), and earned his first major international recognition.

•Helped United win the UEFA Champions League and Premier League titles.

Real Madrid (2009–2018)

•In 2009 he moved to Real Madrid for a then world-record transfer fee.

•At Real Madrid he became the club’s all-time top scorer, dominated La Liga and European competitions, and collected multiple individual honours.

•Won four UEFA Champions League titles (part of a period of dominance for Real) and multiple domestic league titles.

Juventus FC (2018–2021)

•Transferred to Juventus in 2018. Continued to score and won Serie A titles.

Return to Manchester United & Move to Al-Nassr

•Returned to Manchester United in 2021.

•In November 2022, his contract with Manchester United was terminated by mutual agreement.

•In December 2022 he signed with Saudi club Al-Nassr.

•At Al-Nassr (2023 onward), he continues playing at a high level.

International Career with Portugal

•Made his debut for the Portugal senior national team in August 2003.

•Captained Portugal and played in multiple major tournaments: FIFA World Cups, European Championships, UEFA Nations League, etc.

•Key international achievements include leading Portugal to victory in the UEFA European Championship in 2016 — the country’s first major trophy as a national team.

•Became the first male player to score at five different FIFA World Cups.

Records, Style & Legacy

•Ballon d’Or (awarded to the world’s best footballer) — five times.

•Known for versatility: started as a winger, evolved into a prolific forward/striker, combining pace, skill, aerial ability, goal scoring and physical fitness.

•Among top records in club & international football: goals, appearances, trophies. As of 2025, he remains among the leading all-time goal-scorers and has secured 34 major trophies.

•Created brand identity “CR7”, extending influence beyond football — merchandising, endorsements and more.

Off-Field, Business & Personal Profile

•Off the pitch, he is a global brand: through endorsements, personal brand ventures, and businesses.

•His birth-place Funchal (Madeira) houses a museum dedicated to him: Museu CR7, displaying his trophies and memorabilia.

•Known for extreme discipline, professionalism and work-ethic, contributing to his longevity and success.

Significance

Cristiano Ronaldo’s journey—from humble beginnings in Madeira to becoming one of the greatest footballers ever—is defined by exceptional talent, relentless work ethic, adaptability and consistency.
